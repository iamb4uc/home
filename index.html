    • About me

    Hey there, I am b4uc (͠◉_◉᷅ ). I am a computer science student that loves to tinker with gadgets. I love breaking and building stuff, I am also into hacking both hardware and software. I built this website just to let everyone understand the power of minimalism, because this shit loads fast and I know it. It is made to be "suckless" by the nature of its codebase. I only used HTML5 and CSS for this website and no cookies, telemetry, or fancy javascript features since most of us truly do not care about flashy objects that can be used to fingerprint them.

    I am a great nerd when it comes to online privacy and anonymity. I believe that it is our right to be private about the things we do, may it be online browsing the web or offline living our lives. We should not give up to the mass servillance that our government and big tech impose on us. Unless the court orders a warrant for our name for a certain crime, we should not let them monitor our day-to-day tasks of what we do or what we like without our consent.
