Really generic story ig.

    This website is one of those projects. It implements minimalism as its standard, meaning that very few lines of code is used in making this website. This is widely known in the Linux community as the suckless way of creating projects.

    One of my most favorite part of creating this website is that I suck at designing a website so also took some inspiration from the original suckless.org website and made it my own haha, everything else in here is pretty original. It took around 1 month making this completely functional. I hope to keep it updated frequently, along with blogs occasionally. This blogs will range from discussions about my projects, technology related, privacy related and sometimes life related.

    I deeply care about privacy in this modern world of bloated website that don't even require such features like dark mode, unnecessary JavaScript that collect user without consent or either though a big vague Terms and condition so to avoid all this, this website is 100% JavaScript free and can be accessed though any form of browser may it be librewolf or lynx.
