    Q: What operating system do you use?

    Well the answer is pretty easy, its GNU/Linux and BSD. Well to be precise I currently have Arch Linux, Void Linux on my laptop and both of them have their own purpose. I use Arch for Its repo, tho i tried using Artix since I hate systemd I kindda dont like it, I like original distro without it being based on something else. And I use Void for its minimalism, I love runnit, its fast, snappy and very fun to manage.

    Q: What Desktop Environment(DE) or Window Manager(WM) do you use?

    I dont use any desktop environment since I find them bloated with useless shit they market as features and have a billion of dependencies. I use DWM as my main window manager since it is written in C, it is easy for me to customize and understand what each piece of code does also I prefer WM cause these are light weight and are very customizable. But if you want my recomendation on Desktop Environment, you can use xfce4 since the base version is not as bloated as DE such as Gnome or KDE without compromising productivity.

    If you want my DWM configs you can checkout either my DWM(gruvbox_minimal) files or my dotfiles(dracula_DWM) with some additional dotfiles.

    Q: What IDE do you use?

    I dont use IDE's since all of them are either bloated as fuck or just straight up propriatory blobs. I use vim for doing everything, its very customizeable with the features I actually need. I use VimPLUG for my plugin management
